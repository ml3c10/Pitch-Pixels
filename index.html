FOOTBALL JOURNEY

Football has taught me discipline, teamwork, and resilience through every training session and match.

How I Started Football

My journey began in elementary school when I was in fourth grade and played on my school team, back then I didn't know much about the game but I loved it. I made friends and had exceptional trainors who believed in me. I enjoyed running around the fields and playing with my friends. After being selected to play for the congressional athletic meet I realized that this game would be my passion for life. After that I continued to play football in high school up until now in my college years.

Challenges I Faced

The road wasn't always smooth. I struggled with balancing academics and sports, faced injuries that sidelined me for months, and dealt with the pressure of competition. There were times when I questioned if I was good enough, but each challenge taught me valuable lessons about perseverance and mental toughness.

Why I Kept Going

Despite the setbacks, football remained my anchor. The bond with my teammates, the thrill of competition, and the personal growth kept me motivated. Every goal scored, every match won, and even every loss taught me something new about myself and about life. Football became more than just a sport, it became my teacher and my home.

GAMING JOURNEY

Gaming has developed my patience, strategic thinking, and problem-solving skills.

How I Started Gaming

My gaming journey began when my best friend let me borrow his PSP (PlayStation Portable) and I played a game called Patapon. As a kid, I was fascinated by the graphics and gameplay, and I wanted to experience it for myself. I asked my parents to buy me a PSP but unfortunately we didn't have the luxury to buy those kinds of things. Fast forward to when I was in ninth grade, I finally got my hands on a PlayStation 4 and started playing games like GTAV, Red Dead Redemption 2, and God of War.

How Gaming Became Part of My Life

Gaming transformed from a hobby into an essential part of my life when I realized how much it was teaching me. For some maybe it's just a way to pass time, but for me it's the opposite. I wanted time to slow down and enjoy every moment. Gaming taught me patience, strategy, and problem-solving skills that I still use today. It's not just about the games themselves, but the lessons they teach and the memories they create especially when playing with friends.

The Person Behind the Blog

My Story

Who I Am

I'm Dramelyx Queddeng — a football player, console gamer, and storyteller based in Lucban, Quezon, Philippines. I've been playing football since 2016, competing in tournaments and representing my team through wins, losses, and everything in between. Off the pitch, I'm obsessed with story-driven games that challenge how I think and feel.

Why I Started This Blog

I started Pitch & Pixels because I wanted a place to document my journey honestly, not just the highlights, but the grind, the failures, and the lessons. Whether it's breaking down a defending drill that finally clicked or unpacking why a game's ending wrecked me emotionally, this blog is my way of making sense of experiences worth remembering.

What Drives Me

Football taught me discipline, communication, and how to perform under pressure. Gaming taught me patience, empathy, and how to see the world through someone else's story. Together, they've shaped how I approach growth, one match, one mission at a time.

Personal Motto
"Discipline on the field, strategy in the game, growth in life."
